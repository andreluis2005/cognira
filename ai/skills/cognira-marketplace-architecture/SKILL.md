# Cognira Marketplace Architecture

Use this skill for business-surface design decisions.

## Marketplace principles

- Quality beats raw volume.
- Reputation must reflect learner outcomes, not popularity alone.
- Free, donation, and paid models must be distinct in UX.
- Private and public distribution should both be supported.

## Creator score factors

When a ranking model is needed, prefer a composite score built from:

- learner satisfaction
- completion rate
- retention or readiness gain
- question quality signals
- consistency and trust

Avoid relying on a single vanity metric.

## Default monetization ladder

1. free
2. donation-supported
3. paid one-time
4. paid subscription or bundles

## Sponsorship guardrail

Treat sponsorship and crypto incentives as a modular layer.

Do not let reward mechanics distort the learning loop. Reward validated progress, not superficial single-question outcomes.
